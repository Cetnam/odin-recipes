Description

French onion soup, or soupe à l'oignon gratinée, is a classic French dish made with caramelized onions and beef stock or broth. The soup is topped with bread and cheese, then gratinéed to rich, gooey perfection.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cups sliced onions
  • 5 cups beef broth
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices French bread
  • 4 slices provolone cheese
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese, diced

Steps

  1. Thin Slice onions and caramelized to golden brown
  2. Add broth and seasonings once onions are ready boil and simmer for 30 minutes
  3. Prepare bread slice thick and toast lightly
  4. Once soup is done add to bowl top with bread and cheese and broil for 2-3 minutes
